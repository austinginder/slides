Maintenance is a huge deal

Lots of services out there solving this pain point. Google search "WordPress Maintenance Services"...

What is Maintenance?

  • Cost to keep something up and running.
  • Typically the less desirable or messy work.
Everything requires some maintenance
in order to run smooth.

🚗 🏠 ✈

A WordPress website is no different.
Due to the open nature of WordPress, you, the website owner, can't escape some level of responsility in regards to WordPress maintenance.

To understand why...

Let's explore the vast world of WordPress maintenance

WordPress is Open Source

The ecosystem is not locked down 🔐
🗺

WordPress is Open Source

Advantages

  • Open for developers to learn
  • Control and ownership
  • Lowers barriers
🗺

WordPress is Open Source

Disadvantages

  • Difficult to make the ecosystem feel polished
  • Includes some rough edges
If open systems aren't as polished as closed systems, Why free and open source?

For me the advantages are non-negotiable.

Every platform which remains closed will not survive long term where open platforms are allowed to compete.
